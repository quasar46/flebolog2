Endovasal laser coagulation (EVLC/ЭВЛО)

Non-surgical method for removing saphenous veins

Destruction of the vascular wall by heating it with a laser from the inside, which leads to complete dissolution of the vein in 6-12 months.

Description

“Gold standard” of the treatment of varicose veins of the legs

After removing the damaged vein with a laser, the reverse flow of blood stops and blood circulation is restored.

Bruises (hematomas) and pigmentation go away on their own within a few months.

Without anesthesia

Local anesthesia around the vein (tumescent anesthesia). Does not cause allergies and has no contraindications for the patient. Might be a bit unpleasant.

Without hospitalization

The time spent in the clinic is no more than 2 hours. The procedure itself takes about 40 minutes. After the procedure, the patient has the usual way of life with minimal restrictions.

No cuts or seams

Only one skin puncture is required to insert the laser light guide into the vein. No wounds. No limb care required. The next day you can wash your legs.

No compression stockings

Moderate pain at the injection sites, which is easily relieved by taking non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (Ibuprofen).
